Our Mission

AHEI takes the initiative to provide grants to community-based organizations delivering health education programs and projects.

We focus on critical issues including:

  • The opioid epidemic
  • Social determinants of health
  • Food insecurity
  • Healthcare workforce mental and physical well-being

Grant Focus
